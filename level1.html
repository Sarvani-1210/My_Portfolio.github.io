Level 1 Details

It is simple text-based and contains a username and simple password. While registering User creates a simple text format password that contains the alphanumeric characters along with the special characters. Simple text-based Passwords are

  • It should contain at least 10 characters long.
  • At least one charter should be a capital letter.
  • At least one special character must be used.
  • Should not use a name as a password as it is simple to guess.
